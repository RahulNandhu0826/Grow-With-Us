ABOUT US


Nutritional problems in children constitute the major health problem in developing countries. Children are the keystone of the world. So their better growth impact their future development. Nutrition education plays a fundamental role in encouraging your kids to lead a healthier life. Learning about nutrition might not seem so fun for kids. It is also important to start early, that way when they become adults, they’ll be accustomed to living a healthy lifestyle. Most parents don’t know the basics of keeping children healthy, like offering foods, making sure they get enough sleep and brain development. So we present Grow with us web application developed to bring good health to the children.This mainly focus on health of kids and also providing dietitian for maintaining growth of children at each stages of growth. For children, the right to health is vital because they are vulnerable beings, more at risk to illness and health complications. When children are spared from disease, they can grow into healthy adults, and in this way, contribute to the development of dynamic and productive societies. It improves the health of children from infant to adults. Parents can register their children in this, based on their age group and by applying children’s head circumference, height and weight. That may be infant, toddler or adult. Parents can choose the plans through it. If the parent need a dietitian for their children, they can select and pay for dietitian. Vaccination is essential for the better growth of children’s .So that it is very important to get the vaccination at the correct time. The parent would be able to see all details of vaccination when they register it for the first time. The correct information about the vaccination given to the parents through message notifications. With parents guidance the kids can have fun with activities such as stories, videos and lullaby. It helps in improving brain development and skills for the kids.
